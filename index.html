How We Work

From Raw Pine to Your
Perfect Board or Door

01
Material Selection

Premium 100% pine wood poplar core with Garjan veneer face — carefully selected, seasoned, and treated for optimal moisture content before production begins.

02
Core Preparation

Pine wood strips are precision-cut, kiln-dried and assembled into a uniform, stable core ready for pressing and veneer lamination.

03
Quality Inspection

Every board and door is thoroughly inspected for dimensional accuracy, surface finish, structural strength and moisture levels.

04
Dispatch & Delivery

Carefully packaged and dispatched to your site in Pune and across Maharashtra — on time, every time.
